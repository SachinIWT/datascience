WELCOMEData Scientists

Congratulations for choosing one of the most rewarding careers of 21st Century. The Data Science Book (based on the CBSE curriculum) has been written to make the subject both exciting to study, and easy to understand. The book approaches both theoretical foundations of Data Science and practical knowledge with a lot of examples, fun activities and case studies.

The essential skills of Data Science including Statistics and Probability are seeing a rapid growth in a “universal” sense… but there is a need for a comprehensive understanding of the interplay of these subjects from a practitioner's perspective. Especially when it is touching all aspects of human life.

We have produced this book with a lot of passion, and are looking forward to see you obtain a gripping command over the subject as you progress with the reading.
